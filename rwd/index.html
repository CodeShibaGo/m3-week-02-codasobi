About us

Trusted ingredients and high quality products. With a lineup featuring some of your favorite Earls dishes, you can now find items becoming available in select grocery stores across Canada. Let’s get cooking!

Slow Cooked BBQ Ribs

Earls Slow Cooked BBQ Ribs have finally arrived to make at home! Our addictive pork back ribs are now easy to prepare in your oven or on your grill. These are sure to become a new go-to for quick dinners or when entertaining. Available across Canada at Sobeys, Safeway and Thrifty Foods stores.

Earls Beef Patties

Earls signature beef patties are made with Canadian beef chuck and bring the flavours of the restaurant to your home. Grill your patties on the BBQ or fry on your stovetop, then pair them with your favourite toppings and condiments to make the perfect at-home burger. Available at Sobeys, Safeway, Foodland and Thrifty Foods stores across Canada.

Classic Clam Chowder

Earls famous Clam Chowder can now be enjoyed at home. Our long-standing restaurant favourite is a classic for a reason! Available at participating Sobeys, Safeway and Thrifty Food stores across Western Canada.
